About

I work in urban planning and design, focusing on small-scale plans, schools, and the everyday spaces people actually use. Since graduating from The Azrieli School of Architecture at Tel Aviv University in 2021, I've worked across both private practice and public-sector planning. My experience includes coordinating with consultants and authorities, reviewing statutory projects, and redesigning educational environments to make them more functional, inspiring, and user-focused.
